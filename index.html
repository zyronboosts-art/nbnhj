FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Servitix Solutions.

What services does Servitix Solutions offer?

We specialize in custom website development, web application development, AI software solutions, AI integration into existing systems, mobile-first design, and comprehensive digital transformation strategies. Our team handles everything from concept and design to deployment and ongoing support.

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 2–4 weeks, while custom web applications or AI solutions may take 4–12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout every phase of development.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a modern, cutting-edge technology stack including React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, TensorFlow, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. For AI solutions, we leverage the latest in machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. We select the best tools for each project's specific requirements.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, performance optimization, security updates, and feature enhancements. We offer flexible maintenance plans tailored to your needs, ensuring your product continues to perform at its best long after deployment.

How much does a project typically cost?

Every project is unique, so we provide custom quotes based on your specific requirements. We offer competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a free consultation and we'll provide a detailed proposal with clear milestones and deliverables tailored to your budget.

Can you integrate AI into my existing system?

Yes! AI integration is one of our core specialties. We can seamlessly incorporate AI capabilities—such as chatbots, predictive analytics, recommendation engines, and automated workflows—into your existing infrastructure without disrupting your current operations. We start with a thorough assessment of your systems to ensure smooth integration.

Do you sign NDAs to protect client confidentiality?

Yes, absolutely. We take client confidentiality very seriously. We're happy to sign NDAs before any project discussion begins. Additionally, all clients retain 100% ownership of their intellectual property — there's no lock-in with Servitix Solutions. Your ideas and your code are always yours.
